About Colombo Conferences | Sri Lanka’s Top MICE Specialists Since 2015

About Colombo Conferences, a premier event management company based in Sri Lanka, renowned for excellence. Since 2015, we’ve been transforming ideas into unforgettable experiences, specializing in Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions (MICE).

Located in Colombo, we pride ourselves on delivering comprehensive event solutions for local corporates and international clients, including event companies from India (Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi) and the Middle East (Dubai, UAE). Learn more about Colombo Conferences and our passion for creating impactful events worldwide. Our mission is to make Sri Lanka a top MICE destination by blending creativity, professionalism, and local expertise.



Why Global Brands Trust Colombo Conferences For MICE Events

At Colombo Conferences, we’ve spent over 10 years perfecting the art of unforgettable events. Whether it’s finding the perfect venue, managing logistics, setting up top-notch audiovisual experiences, or keeping your guests engaged, we’ve got it covered. Our goal? To make every event run smoothly and leave a lasting impression.
